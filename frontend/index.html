About Food Connect

Our Mission to End Food Waste and Hunger

Our Story

Food Connect was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to create a world where no one goes hungry while perfectly good food goes to waste. We bridge the gap between food surplus and food insecurity, connecting donors with those in need through our innovative platform.
